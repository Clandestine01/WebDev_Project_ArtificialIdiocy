WHY YOU SHOULD GET OLD MERCEDES BENZ

Prologue

At one point in every boy's life they stop dreaming of having a fleet of new ferraris, overhyping instagram race cars and they start dreaming of not an expensive car but a car with a soul

That is when a boy becomes a man, he get's himself a companion. And while for every person there is a different car in this psychotic episode we will look on the timeless classic of the Mercedes-Benz W124.

Overview

The Mercedes-Benz W124 is a range of mid-size vehicles produced by Mercedes-Benz from 1984 to 1997. While others will yap using fancy words I will say this: This beast is built like a tank(probably from a tank), with great comfort and it's body is a serious of "imma use aerodynamics but im not kneeling for it" definetily gives it a character
