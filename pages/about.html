About myself

As the home page suggested, my name is John! John O'Rielly to be more specific. I founded my own car dealership, Wheels on Fire, in 1998. I'll admit, it was mostly an excuse to buy more cars at the time haha. But over the years, it actually started turning a profit, and I found myself enjoying helping folks out, so I decided to make managing the company my full time job.
